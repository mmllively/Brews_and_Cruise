Karina Drummond

I am a stay-at-home mom to four children (ages 3, 5, 9, and 16), and wife to an active-duty Sailor. I am a first-generation Dominican-American, born and raised in Miami. Before the Navy brought us to San Diego in 2018, I lived in Orlando for 12 years. I completed my Bachelor of Science in Psychology in May 2020 from UCF and graduated, and a Master of Arts in Educational Leadership in August 2022.

I chose to enroll in the UCF Coding Bootcamp because I have always wanted to learn how to develop websites and apps, but was too afraid to take the first step. Four weeks into the bootcamp, and I am so happy I finally made the change! As challenging as it is, I am thoroughly enjoying every moment of it and looking forward to completing the program and starting my career!
